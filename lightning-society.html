The Opportunity

AI is about to transform
live entertainment.

Within three years, AI will be capable of generating real-time music, voice, and video at a quality level indistinguishable from human performance. Venues that build the infrastructure now will own the category. Those that wait will be retrofitting.

Lightning Society has the ideal canvas: three stained glass windows on the staircase, projection surfaces across multiple ballrooms, and public spaces that guests move through continuously. ETHEREA transforms every surface into a living, responsive intelligence that reacts to what's happening in the room.

We're not proposing a one-time installation. We're proposing a partnership where General Semantics serves as your spatial imagination partner, continuously deploying new capabilities as AI models evolve, opening new revenue channels each year, while the cost to run it all drops dramatically.

Technology

Local brain.
Cloud muscle.

A local workstation powered by an NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 handles everything that needs to feel instant: audio reactivity, live video processing, voice transcription, and ambient generation across all surfaces. Frontier cloud compute handles the heavy generative workloads for the one active surface at a time.

As models shrink, the local machine takes on more. By Year 3, cloud is reserved for parallelized frontier experiences and special occasions. The workstation is modular and can be upgraded with newer GPUs as they become available.

  • NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 Blackwell, 96 GB GDDR7
  • Three stained glass window projections
  • Ballroom and public space projection surfaces
  • Microphones and depth cameras for spatial awareness
  • iOS app for full venue control
  • All ambient surfaces run locally at zero marginal cost
