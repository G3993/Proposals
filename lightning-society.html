The Spirit of the Staircase

A monument to
curiosity.

Lightning Society already lives by a powerful idea: gatherings become generators through shared rituals, art, sound, and space. We follow questions more than answers and see exploration as a form of devotion. The staircase makes that philosophy something you can see, hear, and feel.

Every day, a new creative prompt lights up the staircase. A single question, open-ended and playful, designed to unlock a moment of spontaneous creativity from anyone who walks by.

Share a lyric from your favorite song.
Put a frog in a ridiculous place.
Describe your childhood dog.

Microphones embedded in each level capture every response. No app, no QR code, no account. Just your voice and a few seconds of your time. Speak, and the staircase comes alive. Light, color, and motion respond to what you say in real time.

Every response is archived, building a living collection of creative expression that grows richer over time. When the staircase is quiet, it replays fragments from its memory, cycling through voices and visuals from days and weeks past. The archive also feeds back into ETHEREA, so the community's collective imagination becomes a generative source for future performances.

The lobby offers the same interaction in a more open setting. People who want to engage without stopping on the stairs can do it from below, and when no one is on the staircase, the lobby projection keeps the experience alive. Two touchpoints, one living system.

This would be the first permanent generative art installation of its kind in New York, and the most beautiful staircase in the city.

Beyond the Staircase

A creativity amplifier
for your entire space.

The staircase shows the world your values. ETHEREA can do the same for every room in the building. It makes people who don't think of themselves as creative, creative. And it makes the most creative people in the world even more creative.

A poet speaks and the room becomes what they describe. A singer steps on stage alone and suddenly has a full visual band. A DJ's set paints trails of light across every surface. A dancer's gestures become form and color in real time.

For brands, ETHEREA builds entire worlds. A product launch becomes an environment guests walk through. A campaign becomes something people experience with their whole body. From the staircase preview to full-room immersion.

As the system grows in capability, it serves more and more of what brings people into your space. Thursday salons, Saturday headliners, thought leadership panels, immersive celebrations. And because it runs continuously, Lightning Society becomes the first partner on a 24-hour generative live stream, powered by everything your community creates.

Software

The spatial
imagination OS.

Three tools work together to turn every surface in the building into a programmable canvas. Etherea handles real-time AI generation. ShaderClaw handles offline, prerendered content. Easel maps it all onto the physical space and lets you seamlessly swap between the two.

ETHEREA
Real-time AI visuals from voice, music, and motion. Every performance is generated live and will never repeat. This is the engine behind every interactive moment.
SHADERCLAW
Generative graphics engine for prerendered and audio-reactive content. Shaders, 3D scenes, text effects, designed and dialed in ahead of time, ready to run on any surface.
EASEL
Projection mapping app that ties it all together. Map any surface, route any source, swap between Etherea and ShaderClaw mid-show. One interface for the entire building.
