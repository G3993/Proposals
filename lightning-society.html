Hardware

Hybrid compute.

A local workstation runs everything that needs to feel instant. Cloud handles the heavy generative work. As models shrink, more moves local. The machine is modular and upgradeable.

  • NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 Blackwell, 96 GB GDDR7
  • Three stained glass window projections
  • Ballroom and public space projection surfaces
  • Microphones and depth cameras for spatial awareness
  • iOS app for full venue control
  • All ambient surfaces run locally at zero marginal cost
Software

The OS of the
spatial canvas.

Three tools work together to turn every surface in the building into a programmable canvas. Etherea handles real-time AI generation. ShaderClaw handles offline, prerendered content. Easel maps it all onto the physical space — and lets you seamlessly swap between the two.

ETHEREA
Real-time AI visuals from voice, music, and motion. Every performance is generated live and will never repeat. This is the engine behind every interactive moment.
SHADERCLAW
Generative graphics engine for prerendered and audio-reactive content. Shaders, 3D scenes, text effects — designed and dialed in ahead of time, ready to run on any surface.
EASEL
Projection mapping app that ties it all together. Map any surface, route any source, swap between Etherea and ShaderClaw mid-show. One interface for the entire building.
