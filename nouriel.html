A Note
Thank you for Sunday night.

Thanks again for the opportunity to perform on Sunday night. We had a blast co-creating with you, and are thrilled by the opportunity to work together to bring ETHEREA permanently into your beautiful home.

As you have seen, ETHEREA's Imagination Engine is a dynamic, always-evolving creative intelligence that gives anyone the ability to instantly use their words to project their imagination in the space around them. We continue to discover new use cases and audiences for this technology, from musical performances, to animated poetry, to immersive audiovisual theatre, and believe that your prolific hosting will provide a living laboratory to demonstrate its immense potential while entertaining and inspiring your guests.

After some thought, Gonzo and I have concluded that installing a B300 inside an apartment is infeasible. They are only sold in nodes of eight which are soldered together, with cooling and power requirements that exceed residential capacity.

We are considering what it might take to gain access to frontier compute inside the NYC metro area, which will be necessary in order to reduce latency such that we can fulfill our ambition of powering live performances in the city at the highest level of production and reliability. We are exploring the feasibility of building a small cooperative data center inside of the 20,000 square foot tech/art accelerator we are building in North Williamsburg.

In the meantime, we can get started as soon as you are ready with our remote provider, supported in a few key latency-sensitive tasks by NVIDIA's current top of the line professional workstation that can live beautifully in your home. This has the added benefit of allowing us to remain flexible to shifting compute requirements as the model landscape changes.

What follows is our approach, pricing, and next steps.

Spatial Canvas
The space itself becomes the screen.

We will install a combination of ultra short throw projectors, microphones, and cameras throughout your home to enable multi-screen visibility and interactivity at day and night, all centrally controlled via an iOS app.

Every surface responds to what's happening in the room. The system supports three modes of operation, each reacting to audiovisual inputs from the microphones and cameras installed throughout the space.

Active
Speak, and watch your words instantly form worlds around you. Supports free-form play, poetry reading, storytelling, musical performances, and standup comedy. Live captions available.

Ambient
When inactive, ETHEREA autonomously evolves, producing hypnotic visuals that meander through concepts in a variety of aesthetics. Fine-tunable to specific moods, themes, or the style of a specific artist.

Imagination Guide
ETHEREA comes to life with a voice that talks back. An interactive guide that takes you on tours, tells stories, and responds to conversation. Massive potential across education, entertainment, and live performance.
Local Brain
The workstation as sculpture.

The system will be controlled via an RTX PRO 6000, NVIDIA's current top of the line GPU workstation. This will power latency-sensitive workloads including local transcription, audioreactivity, a local language model, as well as a smaller, local version of ETHEREA that will become more capable over time. The workstation is modular, and can be upgraded with newer GPUs as they become available.

As discussed, the workstation can be housed in a commissioned glass-blown aquarium that water-cools the GPU while refracting and magnifying the system's generative visuals through water and glass, resulting in a living sculpture that is simultaneously art object, light source, and the brain of the entire installation. We work with a very talented glass sculptor, Edoardo Cozzani, who would be a fantastic choice to bring this to life.

Cloud Compute
Always on. Always evolving.

Currently, a B300 runs about $5–6/hr, which makes 24/7 operation around $4,500/mo. While current chip scarcity requires us to keep the GPU warm around the clock to guarantee on-demand access, we expect to become more flexible as GPU supply increases.

The cloud handles the heavy generative workloads — frontier model inference, image and video synthesis, and the large-scale computation that makes ETHEREA's visuals possible. The local workstation and cloud GPU work in concert, each handling what it does best.
