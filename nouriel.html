ETHEREA

Permanent Home Installation
I. Why

Sunday night was just the beginning.

Your home becomes more than a space where people gather. It becomes the space where a dinner party turns into an act of collective imagination — where every guest leaves having seen something they've never seen before.

ETHEREA is a creative intelligence that lets anyone project their imagination into the space around them using only their voice. We see your prolific hosting as a living laboratory to demonstrate its potential while entertaining and inspiring your guests.

II. Who

Built for the room you already fill.

No technical skill required. Anyone can walk up and speak, and the space responds. A child discovers it differently than a poet. A musician uses it differently than a comedian. The system adapts to whoever is in the room.

III. How

Making it real.

Compute

A B300 can't live in an apartment — sold only in nodes of eight, with cooling and power that exceed residential capacity. We start with a remote provider, supported by an on-site NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 for real-time tasks. We're also exploring a cooperative data center in our North Williamsburg accelerator for ultra-low latency.

Spatial Canvas

Ultra short throw projectors, microphones, and cameras installed throughout the home. Multi-screen, day and night, controlled from an iOS app.

Local Brain

The RTX PRO 6000 handles transcription, audioreactivity, a local language model, and a smaller local ETHEREA that improves over time. Modular and upgradeable.

Aquarium

The workstation housed in a commissioned glass-blown aquarium — water-cooled, refracting generative visuals through glass. Art object, light source, and brain of the installation. Sculptor: Edoardo Cozzani.

Cloud

B300 at $5–6/hr for frontier inference and generative workloads. 24/7 access at ~$4,500/mo. Costs expected to decrease as supply increases.

Imagination Guide

ETHEREA talks back. Tours, stories, conversation. Our most exciting feature to date.
