ETHEREA
Permanent Home Installation
A living AI environment that transforms your home into a responsive, generative experience. Speak, and watch your words instantly form worlds around you.
Approach
Your home, reimagined.

ETHEREA's Imagination Engine is a dynamic, always-evolving creative intelligence that gives anyone the ability to instantly use their words to project their imagination in the space around them.

The system will be powered by a combination of cloud GPU compute for heavy generative workloads and a local NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 workstation for latency-sensitive tasks including transcription, audioreactivity, and a local language model. The workstation is modular and can be upgraded as newer GPUs become available.

We will install a combination of ultra short throw projectors, microphones, and cameras throughout the space to enable multi-screen visibility and interactivity, centrally controlled via an iOS app.

Aquarium
The brain as sculpture.

The workstation can be housed in a commissioned glass-blown aquarium that water-cools the GPU while refracting and magnifying the system's generative visuals through water and glass, resulting in a living sculpture that is simultaneously art object, light source, and the brain of the entire installation.

Control
iOS app.

A dedicated iOS app will allow full control of the installation from iPhone or iPad, including mode selection, ambient theme curation, and system management.
