A letter

Built for your world.

Nouriel,

Thanks again for the opportunity to perform on Sunday night. We had a blast co-creating with you, and are thrilled by the opportunity to work together to bring ETHEREA permanently into your beautiful home.

As you have seen, ETHEREA's Imagination Engine is a dynamic, always-evolving creative intelligence that gives anyone the ability to instantly use their words to project their imagination in the space around them. We continue to discover new use cases and audiences for this technology, from musical performances, to animated poetry, to immersive audiovisual theatre, and believe that your prolific hosting will provide a living laboratory to demonstrate its immense potential while entertaining and inspiring your guests.

After some thought, Gonzo and I have concluded that installing a B300 inside an apartment is infeasible. They are only sold in nodes of eight which are soldered together, with cooling and power requirements that exceed residential capacity.

We are considering what it might take to gain access to frontier compute inside the NYC metro area, which will be necessary in order to reduce latency such that we can fulfill our ambition of powering live performances in the city at the highest level of production and reliability. We are exploring the feasibility of building a small cooperative data center inside of the 20,000 square foot tech/art accelerator we are building in North Williamsburg.

In the meantime, we can get started as soon as you are ready with our remote provider, supported in a few key latency-sensitive tasks by NVIDIA's current top of the line professional workstation that can live beautifully in your home. This has the added benefit of allowing us to remain flexible to shifting compute requirements as the model landscape changes.

What follows is our approach, pricing, and next steps.

James & Gonzo

Local Brain

The workstation
as sculpture.

Powered by an RTX PRO 6000 — NVIDIA's current top of the line GPU. Local transcription, audioreactivity, a local language model, and a smaller, local version of ETHEREA that will become more capable over time. Modular and upgradeable.

The workstation lives inside a commissioned glass-blown aquarium that water-cools the GPU while refracting and magnifying the system's generative visuals through water and glass. A living sculpture that is simultaneously art object, light source, and the brain of the entire installation. Crafted by glass sculptor Edoardo Cozzani.
