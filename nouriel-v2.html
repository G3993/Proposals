A letter

Thank you for this opportunity.

Nouriel,

Thanks again for the opportunity to perform on Sunday night. We had a blast co-creating with you, and are thrilled by the opportunity to work together to bring ETHEREA permanently into your beautiful home.

As you have seen, ETHEREA's Imagination Engine is a dynamic, always-evolving creative intelligence that gives anyone the ability to instantly use their words to project their imagination in the space around them. We continue to discover new use cases and audiences for this technology, from musical performances, to animated poetry, to immersive audiovisual theatre, and believe that your prolific hosting will provide a living laboratory to demonstrate its immense potential while entertaining and inspiring your guests.

After some thought, we have concluded that installing frontier GPUs inside an apartment is infeasible. They are only sold in multi-unit configurations with cooling and power requirements that exceed residential capacity. Even if it were possible, the economics would only start to make sense as you approach 24/7 utilization. For these reasons, we recommend a hosted model where ETHEREA manages the cloud infrastructure on your behalf, paired with a powerful local workstation that will power latency-sensitive workloads and become increasingly capable of running models locally over time. Two years of unlimited cloud compute are included. This also ensures your installation is always running the most capable models available, without being locked into any single piece of hardware.

What follows is our approach, pricing, and next steps.

James & Gonzo

Spatial Canvas

A Living Canvas for
Unlimited Creativity.

ETHEREA's Imagination Engine will utilize projectors, cameras, and microphones placed throughout your home to transform speech, motion, and music into a limitless creative canvas. All controlled via an iOS app.

Speak, and watch your words instantly form worlds around you, always powered by the most state of the art intelligence. This mode supports both unstructured, free-form play like what we delivered on Sunday night, as well as more intentional settings like poetry reading, storytelling, musical performances, and even standup comedy.
Every session is recorded locally, building a living memory of everything imagined in the space. When the system is idle, ETHEREA draws from this memory, autonomously evolving and remixing past creations into hypnotic new visuals.
The Imagination Guide brings ETHEREA to life as a creative partner you can talk to. It can improvise a story, guide you through ancient Rome, or run a two-hour scripted performance, all while painting the world around you in real time.
The Brain

Always at the frontier.

ETHEREA runs on frontier cloud compute for heavy generative workloads and an NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 locally for everything that needs to feel instant. ETHEREA manages the cloud infrastructure on your behalf, with a dedicated GPU that spins up on demand. Two years of unlimited cloud compute are included.

The system ships fully functional with smaller models running locally on delivery, and as AI continues to get smaller and more efficient, we will keep working to bring more capabilities onto your local machine, making the system increasingly autonomous from the cloud over time. The workstation is modular and can be upgraded as newer GPUs become available.

The workstation can be hidden and integrated into your existing AV infrastructure, or housed in a commissioned glass-blown aquarium that water-cools the GPU while refracting and magnifying the system's generative visuals through water and glass, resulting in a living sculpture that is simultaneously art object, light source, and the brain of the entire installation. We work with a very talented glass sculptor, Edoardo Cozzani, who would be a fantastic choice to bring this to life.
